Ashley's Journey: Building a Digital Empire

Ashley knew from a young age that she wanted to create something of her own. Ashley is from a small suburban town outside of Chicago, accrediting her desire to connect people and ideas from around the world to her small-town roots.

Ashley found herself drawn to technology and programming in her early twenties. She has spent countless hours teaching herself coding languages, and bulding her own websites. Her passion for creating online content has continued to grow, which has led to her enrollment in a plethora of front-end development courses.

Today, Ashley pours her heart and soul into website development. Ashley is determined to expand her business, and is hopeful to meet the needs of each and every client.
